OUR RESPONSIBILITIES

How We Represent You

The Student Advisory Committee (SAC) is a student group established within each academic unit to promote communication and interaction among students, faculty, staff, and administrators. SACs are both a social and academic organization within their academic unit, and as such, they advance involvement opportunities, encourage participation in departmental activities, and work together with others to build campus community.

FAQ

Problems Accessing Your Ballot?

To protect the accuracy of the election results. The election ballot requires students to sign into the University's Active Directory to record their vote. Sometimes this can be difficult, so some instructions are here. If you have any problems, please reach out to SAC@music.utah.edu. 

 

Who Should Vote?

All students who have a declared program in the School of Music should vote!
 

Why Should I Vote?

Voting in the SAC election gives you the opportunity to choose representatives who will voice your concerns and ideas within the School of Music. Your vote helps shape the direction and initiatives of the SAC, ensuring that student perspectives are considered in decision-making processes.
 

What Happens After the Election?

Once the voting period concludes, the ballots will be counted, and the candidates with the highest number of votes will be elected as representatives to the Student Advisory Council. Elected representatives will then begin their terms at the start of the 2025 â€“ 2026 Academic Year. 
 

When is the Deadline to Cast Votes?

The deadline to submit your vote will be on Monday, 5 May 2025 at 11:59 PM!

Results will be announced on 6 May 2025.
